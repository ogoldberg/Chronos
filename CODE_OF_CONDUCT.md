# Code of Conduct

Chronos is a welcoming community. Be kind, be useful, and assume good
faith.

We follow the [Contributor Covenant 2.1][cc]. In short:

- Be respectful. Disagree about ideas, not people.
- No harassment, personal attacks, or bigoted language of any kind.
- Prefer concrete technical discussion over generalities.
- When you're wrong, say so. When someone else is wrong, show them
  why — with code, data, or a link.

Report conduct issues to `conduct@chronosapp.org`. Reports are
confidential and handled by the maintainers.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that don't
align with this code, and may ban contributors for sustained
violations.

[cc]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
